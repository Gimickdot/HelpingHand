💡 Pro Tips for Learning ASL

  • Practice in front of a mirror to check your hand positions
  • Start with the most common letters first (A, E, I, O, S, T, R, N)
  • Keep your hand relaxed but firm - don't strain your fingers
  • Practice signing your name and simple words
  • Watch videos of native ASL signers to learn proper form
  • Sign slowly at first, then gradually increase your speed
  • Practice daily for 15-20 minutes for best results

🎯 Practice Routine

  1. Start with hand stretches to warm up your fingers
  2. Practice each letter of the alphabet slowly and deliberately
  3. Spell out simple 3-letter words (CAT, DOG, SUN)
  4. Try spelling your own name multiple times
  5. Practice common signs like HELLO and THANK YOU
  6. Test yourself by trying to sign without looking
  7. Use the Practice Mode to test your recognition skills
  8. Challenge yourself with the ASL Game when ready!
